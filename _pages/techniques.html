Services & Techniques

Noble Choice Chiropractic strives to make patients feel comfortable while receiving the best treatment possible. To ensure this, the chiropractors at Noble Choice Chiropractic provide various up-to-date techniques for all patients. These include:

Diversified Technique

This technique is the primary form of adjusting taught at many of the major chiropractic colleges world-wide. The technique is delivered through a hands-on approach where the chiropractor adjusts a specific spinal segment (subluxation) to restore correct motion and remove any neurological interference.

Drop Piece Adjusting

Drop Technique is an extension of diversified technique where parts of the adjusting table give way while the chiropractor applies pressure to a specific spinal segment (subluxation) to restore correct motion and remove any neurological interference.

Rehabilitation

The chiropractors at Noble Choice Chiropractic tailor exercises to your specific care. These exercises are geared to help return you to your optimal performance. Exercises range from simple movement with no weight to help regain your full range of motion, to weighted exercises to help strengthen areas that are weak.
