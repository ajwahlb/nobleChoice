What to expect

At Noble Choice Chiropractic, our chiropractors strive to develop a relationship based on trust and honesty. During the first visit, a new patient will complete the paperwork to provide Dr. Steph and Dr. Jon the necessary information about your specific condition and health history. You will then have a consultation with Dr. Steph and Dr. Jon to discuss your health history, specific condition and any questions you may have. After your consultation, the doctors will then perform a thorough orthopedic and neurologic exam and take a complete set of x-rays.

Questions

The doctors will correlate all the information gained from the consultation, exam and x-rays to complete a Report of Findings. Most patients have four main questions:

  1. What is wrong with me/Can you help me?
  2. What are you going to do?
  3. How long will it take?
  4. How much will it cost?
If you can be helped by chiropractic, the doctors will answer all your questions and discuss your individual treatment plan. If Dr. Steph and Dr. Jon believe that you are not a chiropractic candidate, they will refer you to the appropriate health care professional.
